Chicken Biryani
Ingredients
  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 1 kg Chicken pieces
  • Yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, biryani spices, onions, saffron
Procedure
  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices & salt for 1‚Äì2 hours.
  2. Fry sliced onions until golden. Prepare masala with tomatoes and spices.
  3. Par-cook rice with whole spices until 70% done.
  4. Layer rice & chicken in a heavy pot, sprinkle saffron milk, seal and cook on low (dum) for 20‚Äì25 mins.
  5. Gently mix and serve hot with raita.
